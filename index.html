A little bit about myself

Djimitry has always wanted to tell stories through visual media. After his graduation, Djimitry turned his interest toward Graphic Design and Web Design and Development. He is self-taught in HTML, CSS (SASS/SCSS), JavaScript (Pure and jQuery), and Python. He desires to further improve his design skills, as well as increase his Web Development knowledge.
