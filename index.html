About Me

I'm a self-taught designer and developer in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python; eagerly learning React, Flask, and MongoDB. I love discovering more about the Node.js (expecially Express.js) technology, as well as different CSS libraries, such as Bootstrap, Bulma, and other less known libraries.


I am currently based in the beautiful southwestern desert of New Mexico, USA, enjoying the warm sun and the mountains. If I am not designing, coding, or studying, I am usually out and about the downtown area of Las Cruces or reading.
